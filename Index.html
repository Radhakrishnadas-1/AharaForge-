AharaForge

Healthy Eating Made Simple

AharaForge helps families and busy professionals plan personalized meals, reduce food waste, and build healthy habits — powered by AI and designed for Indian kitchens.

Quick facts:
  • Target: Urban professionals & families (18–40)
  • Core tech: AI recommendation engine, Firebase backend
  • Primary revenue: Subscription model (launch later)

Problem & Solution

Focus: Health + Waste
Problem: Many urban families and professionals struggle to maintain consistent, personalized diets. This leads to poor health outcomes and avoidable household food waste.

Solution: AharaForge provides AI-powered personalized meal plans, practical recipes, meal reminders, and a food-waste tracker that suggests reuse ideas — reducing waste and improving health.

Everything about AharaForge (Full Product Summary)

Vision: To help every household eat healthier with less waste — combining nutrition science, AI, and simple habit design.

Target Customers: Urban working professionals and young families (18–40). Secondary: college students and fitness enthusiasts.

Core Technology: AI recommendation engine (OpenAI/Gemini), Firebase backend (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions), and mobile frontend (React Native/Expo or Flutter).

Business Model: Freemium: core planning and tracking free; premium subscription unlocks advanced AI personalization, grocery lists, and expert tips. Later revenue channels: partnerships, white-labeling for corporates.

Data & Privacy: Stores minimal personal info (preferences, goals). Keys and AI calls are server-side (Cloud Functions) to protect API keys. Users can request data deletion.

Validation: Prototype tested with early users (aim for 50+ testers). Use qualitative interviews and in-app metrics to measure engagement and willingness to pay.

Roadmap:
  • Phase 1: Core app with meal plans, reminders, waste tracker
  • Phase 2: AI personalization improvements and partnerships
  • Phase 3: Scale via B2B pilots (schools, workplaces) and paid tiers
